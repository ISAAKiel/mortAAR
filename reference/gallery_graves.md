# gallery_graves: Life table example

A dataset from *Czarnetzki 1966*.

## Format

A data frame with 128 rows and 4 variables.

- **indnr:** ID of the individual represented by a skull.

- **sex:** sex determination, w -\> female, m -\> male.

- **age:** age class.

- **site:** collective grave.

## Details

Skulls from four different collective graves. This dataset is not well
prepared and needs some adjustment.

## References

Czarnetzki A (1966). “Die menschlichen Skelettreste aus vier
neolithischen Steinkisten Hessens und Niedersachsens.” Dissertation at
the University of Tuebingen.
